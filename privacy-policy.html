Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mielmochi Apps ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Voidfarers ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Voidfarers does not collect any personally identifiable information directly. All game progress (run history, unlocked components, Void Shards) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Play Services
The App is distributed via Google Play and is subject to Google Play's terms and privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. Advertising

The App displays optional rewarded advertisements powered by Google AdMob. These ads are optional — they are only shown when you choose to watch them (e.g. to recover your ship or receive emergency fuel). We do not show forced interstitial ads.

4. Data Storage

All game data including your progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. This data is not transmitted to or stored on any external servers operated by Mielmochi Apps.

5. Children's Privacy

Voidfarers is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
